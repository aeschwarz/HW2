I am originally from Darien, IL, which is a Southwest Suburb of Chicago. I went to Hinsdale South High School and played football and baseball while I was there. I have one brother (Matt, 23) and one sister (Lindsey, 19). My first job was working for Panera Bread; doing anything from making the food to washing dishes (not at the same time obviously). After I quit there, I started working for a small sports retail company, Pro Jersey Sports. I still currently work there during school breaks and summers. Also, for the past three summers I have worked for my city's public works department. I started my first job on campus here this fall. I am working for the CRWC at the membership services desk. I am the one who checks your ID's in order to gain access to the Rec (tough I know).

I have grown up my whole life surrounded by sports. Throughout my life I have played baseketball, baseball, football, and hockey. I have never played soccer before and that is something that I take pride in. Hockey is without a doubt my favorite sport. The Chicago Blackhawks are my favorite team and in my opinion have the best logo in all of sports. I would have to say that football comes next, followed by baseball and basketball. I am a Chicago fan for every team except for the Cubs.

For fun I like to hangout with friends, watch tv, play videogames, workout. As of right now my favorite show is Breaking Bad and I am not really sure what I am going to do once it's over. My favorite video game is the NHL series, I usually buy the updated version every September. Lately, I have been playing a lot of Grand Theft Auto 5. I should have waited to buy it though so that I can get all of this work done first. I also like playing Madden and Call of Duty.
